Our Story

More Than Just Furniture

Founded in 2021, CQC Business Solutions brings over 28 years of collective industry experience to the South African office furniture market. We were born from a vision to provide quality, affordable office solutions while addressing the critical gap in after-sales service and refurbishment.

In an industry dominated by cheap imports with no spare parts or warranties, we identified an opportunity to put money back into our clients' pockets by restoring and refurbishing their existing furniture. Today, we're a Level 1 B-BBEE contributor with a national footprint, serving everyone from home offices to corporate giants like Discovery, Standard Bank, and MTN.
