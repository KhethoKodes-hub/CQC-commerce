Our Journey

28 Years of Excellence

A legacy built on quality, trust, and transformation.

1993 – 2020

Decades of Industry Expertise

Our founding team accumulated decades working alongside major manufacturers and corporate clients across South Africa — forging the knowledge that powers CQC today.

2021

CQC Business Solutions Founded

Launched with a bold vision to provide premium office furniture and expert refurbishment services to South African businesses of every size.

Launching 3D product visualisation, an enhanced e-commerce experience, and expanded service offerings across the African continent. The future is bright.
